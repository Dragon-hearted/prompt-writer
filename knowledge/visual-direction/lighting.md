# Lighting Reference

Model-agnostic reference for lighting in AI-generated images and video. Lighting sets mood more powerfully than any other visual element. This guide covers how to describe lighting in prompts so generation models produce intentional, cinematic results.

---

## Core Principle

**Describe how light behaves, not just what it is.** "Golden hour lighting" is a start. "Golden hour sunlight from the side and slightly behind him, casting long warm shadows on the concrete, soft rim light on his shoulders and hair edges" gives the model enough to produce something cinematic.

Three dimensions of lighting to specify in every prompt:
1. **Direction** — Where the light comes from relative to the subject
2. **Quality** — Hard (sharp shadows) vs soft (diffused, wrapped)
3. **Interaction** — How light hits specific surfaces in the scene

---

## Lighting Moods

### Golden Hour

Warm orange-amber light with long directional shadows. The most requested and versatile lighting mood for lifestyle and aspirational imagery.

**Best for:** Lifestyle, outdoor scenes, warm emotional moments, aspirational imagery, product shots with warmth.

**Prompt keywords:** "golden hour", "warm sunset light", "long shadows", "amber cast", "warm directional light"

**Interaction phrasing:**
- "Golden hour sunlight from the side and slightly behind him, casting long warm shadows on the concrete"
- "Soft rim light on his shoulders and hair edges"
- "The cream fabric glows warm in the golden hour light"
- "The blue stripes catch directional golden hour light creating a subtle shimmer"

---

### Blue Hour

Cool moody twilight — the period just after sunset or before sunrise. Deep blue shadows with practical lights (neon, streetlights, windows) providing warm accent.

**Best for:** Sophisticated, premium, evening scenes, urban settings, cinematic mood.

**Prompt keywords:** "blue hour", "twilight", "cool ambient light", "deep blue shadows", "neon/practical lights pop"

**Interaction phrasing:**
- "Cool blue twilight ambient with warm streetlight pools on the wet pavement"
- "Deep blue shadows on the face, warm accent from a storefront window"

---

### Hard / Direct Light

High contrast with sharp, well-defined shadows. Creates drama and bold visual statements.

**Best for:** Fashion editorial, bold campaigns, dramatic product shots, graphic compositions.

**Prompt keywords:** "hard directional light", "sharp shadows", "high contrast", "strong single source"

**Interaction phrasing:**
- "Hard directional light from the upper left, casting sharp diagonal shadows across the wall"
- "High contrast — deep shadows on one side of the face, bright highlight on the other"

---

### Soft / Diffused Light

Even, gentle illumination that wraps around the subject. Flattering and approachable.

**Best for:** Beauty, skincare product shots, approachable lifestyle, soft portraits.

**Prompt keywords:** "soft diffused light", "even lighting", "gentle", "soft shadows", "wrapped light"

**Interaction phrasing:**
- "Soft, even light that wraps around the subject with minimal shadows"
- "Diffused overcast light — no harsh shadows, muted but flattering"

---

### Backlit

Light source behind the subject, creating rim light, silhouette edges, and potential lens flare.

**Best for:** Hero shots, dramatic reveals, silhouette moments, aspirational imagery.

**Prompt keywords:** "backlit", "rim light", "halo glow", "silhouette", "lens flare"

**Interaction phrasing:**
- "Backlit by late afternoon sun — bright rim of light around the subject's hair and shoulders"
- "Silhouette against a bright window, edges glowing"
- "Strong backlight creating a halo effect around the subject"

---

### Studio Flat

Even, shadowless illumination on a clean background. Professional, commercial, e-commerce standard.

**Best for:** Product on white, e-commerce images, clean commercial shots, isolated product photography.

**Prompt keywords:** "studio lighting", "white background", "no shadows", "clean studio", "even flat light"

**Interaction phrasing:**
- "Clean studio flat lighting — product evenly lit on pure white background, no shadows"
- "Professional product photography lighting — even, neutral, no color cast"

---

### Neon / Colored Light

Vibrant, saturated light from colored sources. Energetic and futuristic feel.

**Best for:** Tech products, youth-oriented campaigns, nightlife, gaming aesthetics, music/entertainment.

**Prompt keywords:** "neon glow", "colored lighting", "vibrant", "RGB", "cyberpunk", "saturated light"

**Interaction phrasing:**
- "Purple and cyan neon light reflecting off wet pavement"
- "Subject lit by alternating red and blue neon from either side"
- "Neon sign glow casting colored reflections on the product surface"

---

### Natural Window Light

Side-lit from a window, organic and authentic. The most common natural indoor lighting setup.

**Best for:** Interior lifestyle, home settings, authentic feel, casual portraits.

**Prompt keywords:** "window light", "natural side lighting", "organic", "indoor natural light"

**Interaction phrasing:**
- "Soft window light from the left, creating gentle shadows on the right side of the face"
- "Natural light streaming through floor-to-ceiling windows, illuminating the desk from the side"
- "Morning golden light through sheer curtains, diffused and warm"

---

### Moody / Low-Key

Mostly shadow with selective pools of light. Dramatic, luxurious, cinematic.

**Best for:** Luxury brand imagery, mystery, cinematic mood, premium products, editorial drama.

**Prompt keywords:** "low-key lighting", "dramatic shadows", "moody", "pools of light", "chiaroscuro"

**Interaction phrasing:**
- "Low-key lighting — most of the frame in shadow, a single pool of warm light on the subject's face and hands"
- "Gallery track lighting from above creating warm directional pools against dark surroundings"
- "Dark, moody atmosphere with selective illumination on the product"

---

### High-Key

Bright, minimal shadows, clean and uplifting. Communicates happiness, cleanliness, and openness.

**Best for:** Happy campaigns, clean corporate imagery, health/wellness, bright product shots.

**Prompt keywords:** "bright even lighting", "high-key", "clean", "minimal shadows", "bright and airy"

**Interaction phrasing:**
- "High-key lighting — bright, even, minimal shadows throughout"
- "Bright and airy atmosphere, light flooding the entire scene"

## Time-of-Day Lighting Strategies

| Time | Visual Effect | Color Temperature | Shadow Character |
|------|---------------|-------------------|-----------------|
| **Golden hour** | Warm, directional, long shadows | Orange/amber cast | Long, soft-edged, warm |
| **Blue hour** | Cool, moody, deep blue shadows | Cool blue with warm accent | Subtle, deep blue tones |
| **Noon** | Harsh overhead, minimal shadows, flat but bright | Neutral white | Short, sharp, directly below |
| **Overcast** | Soft, even, no harsh shadows, muted palette | Neutral to cool | Minimal, very soft |
| **Night** | Dark, high contrast, practical light sources dominant | Mixed — warm practicals, cool ambient | Deep, hard-edged from point sources |
| **Morning (pre-golden)** | Clean, fresh, gentle directional | Slightly warm, clean | Medium-length, soft |

## Light-Subject Interaction

The highest-impact lighting technique is describing how light **interacts with specific materials** in the scene.

### Fabric and Clothing

- "The cream fabric glows warm in the golden hour light"
- "The blue stripes catch directional light creating a subtle shimmer"
- "Matte cotton absorbing the soft light with no reflections"
- "Silk catching a hard highlight along its draped edge"

### Skin

- "Warm light on fair skin, subtle golden tones"
- "Soft rim light separating the subject from the dark background"
- "Directional side-light creating gentle shadow contouring on the cheekbones"

### Metal and Glass

- "The matte black bottle catches a subtle highlight along its curved edge"
- "Watch face reflecting a tiny pinpoint of golden light"
- "Glass bottle refracting the background into soft colored shapes"

### Environment

- "Warm light pools on the concrete floor from overhead track lighting"
- "Long warm shadows stretching across the basketball court surface"
- "Dappled light through tree leaves creating organic patterns on the ground"

## Key / Fill / Rim Terminology

Use these terms selectively when the lighting setup matters to the scene:

| Term | Role | Description | When to Specify |
|------|------|-------------|-----------------|
| **Key light** | Primary, dominant | The main light source that shapes the scene | When the direction and character of the main light are important |
| **Fill light** | Softens shadows | Secondary light reducing contrast from the key | Usually implied by "soft shadows" rather than named explicitly |
| **Rim light** | Edge separation | Light from behind/side that creates a bright edge around the subject | When the subject needs to separate from the background, especially in dark scenes |
| **Practical light** | In-scene source | A visible light source within the scene (lamp, neon sign, candle) | When the light source itself is a visual element in the composition |

**Prompt phrasing:**
- "Gallery track lighting from above-left casts a warm directional pool of light on the model" (key)
- "Soft fill from the right preventing harsh shadows" (fill)
- "Soft rim light on his shoulders and hair edges, separating him from the background" (rim)
- "A warm desk lamp provides practical light from the left" (practical)

## Mood Through Lighting Quick Reference

| Desired Mood | Lighting Recipe | Key Prompt Phrase |
|-------------|----------------|-------------------|
| Warm and inviting | Golden hour, warm temperature, soft shadows | "Warm golden light, soft directional shadows" |
| Moody and dramatic | Strong directional, deep shadows, warm pools against dark | "Dramatic directional light, deep shadows, selective illumination" |
| Clean and aspirational | Even, bright, minimal shadows, neutral-to-cool | "Bright, clean, even lighting with minimal shadows" |
| Intimate | Low key, shallow pools of light, surrounding darkness | "Low key — selective warm pools of light in surrounding darkness" |
| Energetic | High contrast, possibly colored, dynamic direction | "High contrast lighting, bold shadows, dynamic direction" |
| Premium / Luxury | Low-key, controlled, possibly backlit rim | "Moody controlled lighting, rich shadows, subtle rim light" |
| Authentic / Documentary | Natural window or available light, handheld feel | "Natural available light, organic shadows, documentary feel" |
